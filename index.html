2024 Achievements

23,789 new companies registered in FY 2022/23 with total revenue of UGX 77.14 billion. Complete digitization achieved in July 2024 with mobile money payment integration.

Uganda Revenue Authority

Tax Collection & Compliance Authority

Agency Overview

URA is Uganda's premier revenue collection agency, responsible for tax administration, customs services, and trade facilitation. Under Commissioner General John Musinguzi Rujoki, URA achieved historic performance in 2024/25, collecting Shs31.3 trillion and achieving the first budget surplus in nearly a decade.

Historic Achievement 2024/25

Shs31.3 trillion collected (100.84% of target) with a Shs100 billion surplus - the first turnaround in nearly 10 years.

Capital Markets Authority Uganda

Capital Markets Regulation & Development

Agency Overview

CMA is Uganda's capital markets regulator, overseeing securities trading, investment management, and collective investment schemes. Under CEO Josephine Okui Ossiya (appointed February 2024), CMA has seen remarkable growth in the collective investment schemes market, which expanded from Shs 2.8 trillion to Shs 4.2 trillion by March 2025.

Market Growth 2024-2025

Collective investment schemes market grew from Shs 2.8 trillion to Shs 4.2 trillion with fund accounts increasing by 12.8%.
